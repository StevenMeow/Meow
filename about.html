    • About Tower of God

    Tower of God (신의 탑 (in hanja 神의 塔), sinui tap, literally "Tower of God") is a Manhwa series created by the Korean artist SIU that takes place as a Talse Uzer Story. The story is centered around a boy named Twenty-Fifth Baam who chases Rachel his childhood friend who wants to climb to the top of the mysterious Tower.

    Tower of God has been running weekly on Naver since 2010 and is currently comprised of three seasons, Part I, Part II and Part III. The ongoing webtoon is released every Sunday and the official English version is released roughly 14 hours later at WEBTOON.

    Tower of God got adapted into an anime series that ran from April 1, 2020 to June 24, 2020. Its first season contains 13 episodes and adapted Tower of God: Part 1. The second season ran from July 7th, 2024 to December 29th, 2024 and contains 26 episodes, adapting Tower of God: Part 2 - The Prince of Zahard and Tower of God: Part 2 - The Workshop Battle.

    Tower of God gained its first spin-off in 2025, after the conclusion of the third season. It tells the journey of Urek Mazino before and during his climb of the Tower. The ongoing webtoon is released every Monday and the official English version is released roughly 11 hours later at WEBTOON.

    Plot Summary

    Twenty-Fifth Baam is a young man who has never experienced society and the presence of others except for Rachel - the only girl he ever cared for and about. He desired nothing more than to continue living with her. However, when Rachel wants to reach the top of the Tower, she embarks on her journey with Baam desperately following in her tracks...
